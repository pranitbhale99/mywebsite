About cocovriksha

Nurturing Nature with CocoVriksha

Founded in 2022, CocoVriksha is an eco-conscious brand dedicated to creating innovative and sustainable products made from natural coconut coir. We believe in harnessing the power of nature to craft solutions that are not only functional but also kind to the environment.

At cocovriksha, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to produce a wide range of eco-friendly, biodegradable, and durable coir-based products. Every item we make reflects our commitment to sustainability, quality, and a greener future.

From raw material selection to the final product, we ensure that every step (pranit) of our process aligns with our core values — respect for nature, responsibility towards the planet, and dedication to excellence.
